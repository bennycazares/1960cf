1960 CrossFit

Why 1960 CrossFit?

1960 CrossFit is located in Houston, TX and was founded in 2011 on the fundamental principles of fitness and community. We strive to provide not only the best results by following the constantly varied functional movement metadology made famous by CrossFit founder Greg Glassman, but also creating and atmosphere that will encourage you every step of the way to be the version of yourself. When you walk into our facility you will not feel intimidated but actually greeted at the front door by a friendly face at our front desk and embraced by our community that will help hold you accountable to meet your fitness goals.
